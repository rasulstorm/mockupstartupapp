What is a Digital Twin?

A Digital Twin is a virtual representation of a physical system, process, or asset that uses real-time data and advanced simulations to replicate its functionality. It enables businesses to monitor, analyze, and optimize operations by bridging the gap between the physical and digital worlds. In logistics, Digital Twins provide a detailed view of storage facilities, track inventory, predict equipment performance, and improve decision-making with data-driven insights.
